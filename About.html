About the Website

This website is an informative website made only for academic purposes, with the aim to educate and give information about Quantum Cryptography to those who are interested.

I built this website as a practical part of my diploma thesis, wanting to spread my passion about Quantum Cryptography to the wide public. By gathering and organising the information in this website, the visitor can easily find what he/she is searching for, saving time and understanding the content better since it is prepared in the simpliest way possible.

DISCLAIMER: I want to point out that all the information provided on the website is not of my personal research experience, but is a literature review based on the scholarly articles below:
